I’m an award-winning feature writer covering climate change, endangered species and other science topics. I also write product reviews and guides of home, fitness and outdoor gear.

For a decade, I covered science and technology at CNET and, before that, wrote for the now-defunct SYFY channel/NBC Universal blog DVICE. In addition to writing, I'm also an experienced editor, video host and photographer and have appeared as a guest contributor on podcasts, radio and TV.

I was a board member of the Louisville, KY chapter of the Society of Professional Journalists from 2019-2023, and its treasurer from 2023-2024. I have a master’s degree from the University of Louisville and a bachelor’s degree from Connecticut College, both in international relations.
